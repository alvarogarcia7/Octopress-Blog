// each field is an int (4 bytes)</p>

<p>tm_sec:     Seconds after minute (0 – 59)
tm_min:     Minutes after hour (0 – 59)
tm_hour:    Hours after midnight (0 – 23)
tm_mday:    Day of month (1 – 31)
tm_mon:     Month (0 – 11; January = 0)
tm_year:    Year (current year minus 1900)
tm_wday:    Day of week (0 – 6; Sunday = 0) : offset: 24
tm_yday:    Day of year (0 – 365; January 1 = 0)
tm_isdst:   Positive value if daylight saving time is in effect; 0 if daylight saving time is not in effect; negative value if status of daylight saving time is unknown</p>
